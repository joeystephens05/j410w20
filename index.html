Welcome

With a lively dowtown bar scene, wine country just an uber away, and a state univeristy with a strong social scene, San Luis Obispo, CA has its fair share of alcohol drinkers. Endulging in an alcoholic beverage can be a great way to have a little fun, celebrate something special, or simply make life a little more interesting.
While drinking can be fun in moderation, its important that we educate our community members on how to properly navigate their alcohol consumption and a college culture of binge drinking that surrounds us. This site is here to show you how to consume alcohol, while also keeping yourself safe and healthy.
